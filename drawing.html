Structure Drawing

2015

This series of structure drawing is from a course I attened in China in 2015. The aim of these intensive stucture drawings practice is to know more about the skeleton and muscle of human face. Structure drawing helps me improve my drawing skills in a big picture.

Female frontal

I drew this portrait of female frontal for one of my classmates. It took me two hours to finish it. Through this practice, I started to understand the distribution of bones and muscles on the human face. I also began to have a deeper understanding of the shaping of human features. Especially for young lady's face.

anonymous classmate / 2015

Male Three-quarters

I drew this portrait of male three-quarters for one of my classmates. In this practice, I paid more attention to study the bones and muscles of the male side face. Because it is a profile, drawing the ears properly is very important. In addition, the profile drawing also gave me the opportunity to practice the relationship between the face and neck.
