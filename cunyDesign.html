Cuny Design Website

MMP200

In the project, I was prompted to design a website introduces all the design-related major in CUNY. I needed to research the majors all on my own.This website is responsive design using BOOTSTRAP. The framwork includes respinsive CSS, HTML, templates for navigation, image carousels, dropdowna, and other elements.

Persona of a Transfer Student

Rachael has worked in design group for many years, as both a student member and a leader. She has learned and admired many of her mentors throughout the years. When she thinks of her future, she knows she would like to be involved in design study in schools and communities. She feels she would be building on skills she has already developed. During her high school years and two-year college. These experiences have helped her to recognize the need for great designer.

Porsona of a Return Student

Sally is a single mom who is 35. For work she is a graphic designer in a design company, and has almost no social life. Moreover, she needs to do more work for web and app now. Sally has tremendous design capability, but she short of programming. She is trying to get a degree on that. She believes that it’s her mission to create a better world for her children, and that creating that world exists in each interaction she has. At work and in her personal life, Sally exhibits a high level of integrity and passion.

Persona of a New Student

Shu Li is an international student who is from China. She has taken TOEFL test, but, she didn’t get high enough grade to get into her dream college. Therefore, she wants to study in community college now because she can improve her English in the ESL program of CUNY school. And the college is small class so that professor can focus more on every student. Moreover, she can understand more.
